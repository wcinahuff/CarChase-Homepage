A little bit about us.

We've made our home base on the island of Oahu. Our journey into tech started with a need for change. We find it facinating how people build the websites we all use and love. So, as we explored our options for a career change from truck driving we decided to give web development a go. Once we began learning how to code we were hooked and knew this was our ticket to a better life.

These newly aquired skills opened a door that could never be shut. When we learned how to build our first website, we realized we found something that gave us the freedom & versatility we were looking for. Now we made a full switch to front-end development, where we can use our organization skills, excellent teamwork, and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

Our focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

We pride ourselves on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. We focus on keeping our CSS lean and fast to load, and we make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Design into Code

You can trust us to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match.
